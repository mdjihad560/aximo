Most frequently asked questions

What types of technology solutions do you offer?

This question provides an opportunity for the agency to showcase its range of services, whether it's software development, web design, mobile app development, cybersecurity solutions, or IT consulting.

How long will it take to complete my project?

Clients often want to know the timeline for their project's completion to plan accordingly. The agency should provide a clear estimate based on the project scope, complexity, and resource availability.

What is your pricing structure?

Clients typically inquire about the cost of the services provided by the technology agency. They may want to know if pricing is based on a fixed fee, hourly rate, or project milestones. It's essential for the agency to transparently communicate pricing details and any additional costs that may arise.

How do you ensure the security of our data?

Security is a major concern for clients when outsourcing technology services. The agency can explain its approach to data security, including encryption methods, compliance with industry standards.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Clients may want assurance that the agency will be available for support and maintenance after the project launch. The agency can outline its post-project support services, including software updates.
